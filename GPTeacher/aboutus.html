About us

GPTeacher is a website that supports teachers in navigating the education world amidst the rise of GPT technology. It enables teachers to submit student essays, and our technology generates a confidence score to enhance grading accuracy and efficiency, ultimately saving time and effort.

Why us?

You may wonder why you need GPTeacher when other tools exist for checking plagiarism or grammar errors. The answer is simple: GPTeacher differentiates itself by utilizing cutting-edge artificial intelligence to analyze student essays' content, structure, and style. It goes beyond detecting copied or poorly written text and evaluates the originality, coherence, and creativity of the essays.
